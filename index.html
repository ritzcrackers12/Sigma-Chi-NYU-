About Us

Our mission is to develop values-based leaders committed to the betterment of character, campus and community. In the pursuit of these high ideals, the Sigma Chi Fraternity is able to offer tremendous value in augmenting the collegiate experience and supporting the lifelong journey of each of its members.

We hope to assist brothers in becoming men of character, caring husbands, compassionate fathers and community leaders. Membership in Sigma Chi is life-long; you will run into Sigma Chi Brothers nearly everywhere you go and enjoy the advantages of brotherhood throughout your lifetime.

Opportunities

Fraternity Pillars

🎓

Academic Assistance

The Fraternity encourages and enhances academic performance through a scholarship program that encourages tutoring and study skills education. We strive to consistently out-perform the All-Men's and All-Fraternity averages.

🤝

Diversity & Inclusion

Sigma Chi strives to recruit members who have different temperaments, talents and convictions, and who share the same core values. We are committed to developing and expanding our diverse membership.

💼

Career Development

Alumnus brothers offer real-world education in a variety of career fields; some will offer internships and employment networking.

❤️

Philanthropy

The Fraternity places a strong emphasis on serving others. Philanthropic projects are a major focus of activity for Sigma Chi. We are dedicated to our sponsored charities and making a difference in the world.
